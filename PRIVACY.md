# Trace Privacy Policy

Effective: 7 July 2026. Applies to the Trace MCP server ("Trace") by AtlasFlow Systems (Pty) Ltd.

## The short version

Trace is local-first software. It does not collect, transmit, store, or share your data with us or anyone else. Everything Trace knows about you lives in a folder on your own computer.

## Data collection

Trace collects no data. It has no telemetry, no analytics, no accounts, and makes no network requests of its own.

## Data usage and storage

Your career information (Truth Base, job descriptions, fit scores, generated CVs, cover letters, prep packs, application statuses) is written only to a local folder on your machine: `~/.trace/` by default (configurable via the `TRACE_HOME` environment variable). You can inspect, edit, back up, or delete this folder at any time. Deleting it removes everything Trace knows.

## What leaves your machine

Only what you yourself send in conversation. Trace runs inside an MCP client (such as Claude Desktop); the content you exchange with your AI assistant is handled by that client and its model provider under their own privacy policies. Trace does not add any separate transmission channel.

## Third-party sharing

None. Trace shares no data with third parties because it holds no data and sends none.

## Data retention

Retention is entirely under your control: data persists in your local folder until you delete it. We retain nothing because we receive nothing.
